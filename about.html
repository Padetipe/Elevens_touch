Our Story

Elevating fashion since 2020

The Elevens Touch Journey

Founded in 2020 by fashion visionary Blossom Amarachi Samuel, Elevens Touch began as a small boutique with a big dream: to make available clothings that makes every man and woman feel like the best version of theirselves.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved brand known for its impeccable craftsmanship, attention to detail, and commitment to sustainable luxury.

Today, we continue to honor our founder's vision while innovating for the modern woman who values both style and substance.
